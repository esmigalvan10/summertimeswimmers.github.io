Services

Level 1: Guppies
Ages 3-4
This class will focus on teaching Guppies how to not be afraid of the water. We will work on the following skills: entering water safely, getting face and head wet, blowing bubbles, jumping off sundeck into 3ft deep water, and supported swimming in shallow water.

Level 2: Jellyfish
Age 5
This class will focus on teaching Jellyfish how to float. We will work on the following skills: back float, front float, kickboards to deep end, different kinds of kicks, and underwater swimming.
Level 3: Sea Horse
Ages 6+
This class will focus on teaching Sea Horses how to be efficient in water. We will work on the following skills: kicks, dives, deep end swimming, and treading water in the deep end. Prerequisites are front and back float for 5 seconds.

Level 4: Turtles
Ages 6+
This class will focus on teaching Turtles skill competency. We will work on the following skills: front crawl, back stroke, side stroke, and scissor kick. Prerequisites are treading water in the deep end for 30 seconds.

Level 5: Dolphins
Ages 6+
This class will focus on teaching Dolphins advanced skill competency. We will continue to work on the following skills: front crawl, back stroke, side stroke, scizzor kicks, breaststroke and diving. Prerequisites are doing the front crawl and backstroke the length of the pool.
